Welcome to The Study Spot

Located in the heart of Spokane, The Study Spot is more than just a coffee shop HAHAHAHAHHAHAHAHHA; it’s a place where Gonzaga, Whitworth, Eastern, SCC, and WSU students, as well as the local community, gather to enjoy artisan coffee, gluten-free and vegan snacks, and live music every Thursday. Founded by a Gonzaga grad for her Hogan Entrepreneurial Leadership Program project, we strive to create a space that’s welcoming to all.
